About Us

Youth Aflame Social club is an association established to bring together the Youth of Stolberg-Germany for one common mission and goal. Youth Aflame Social Club was established in 2020 by the Youth Ministry within the community. We target young people between the ages of 13-25 years. It is a very interactive group that helps in the good upbringing of young people

Our Goal

Our goal is to bring young people together so they can grow and become great and wonderful people in the community and the country as a whole

Our Mission

Since the youth needs to be more interactive and active in all aspects of their lives, We established this social club to bring them together so they get to know each other and help each other to grow.
